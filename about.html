Katie Lewis is a graphic designer/creative thinker in San Francisco.

Whether it is a brochure, interface or advertisement, I enjoy pushing the boundaries to find unique, expressive and user-centered design solutions. I’ve got a passion for strong typography and perfectly color-balanced photos.

When I am not pushing pixels, you’ll find me sculpting ceramic planters for my garden, walking my fluffy dog friends at the SF SPCA, or DIY-ing decor for my apartment.
